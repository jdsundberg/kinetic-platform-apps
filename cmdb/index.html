Flagship — How other apps consume CMDB

Any Kinetic app — ITIL, SecOps, Asset Management — can resolve and use CIs through these endpoints:

  • GET /api/cmdb/lookup?name=&fqdn=&ip= — find a CI by any identifier
  • GET /api/cmdb/ci/{class}/{id} — full attributes + relationships
  • GET /api/cmdb/ci/{class}/{id}/impact?depth=3 — what services break if this CI fails
  • GET /api/cmdb/ci/{class}/{id}/dependencies?depth=3 — what this CI depends on
  • POST /api/cmdb/ci — upsert (idempotent on CI Number) with change-log
  • POST /api/cmdb/relate — create or find a typed relationship

Integration examples — Incident detail pages call /impact to populate "Affected Services"; vulnerability scanners call /api/cmdb/ci to register newly discovered assets; change records pull /dependencies to compute blast radius.
