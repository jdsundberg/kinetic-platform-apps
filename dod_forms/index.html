Privacy Act Statement

Authority

10 U.S.C. 655, Servicemembers' Group Life Insurance; 37 U.S.C. 551-559, Savings Deposits; 10 U.S.C. 1475-1480, Death Gratuity; 10 U.S.C. 1481-1490, Recovery, Care, and Disposition of Remains; E.O. 9397 (SSN); and DoD Instruction 1300.18.

Purpose

To designate beneficiaries for death gratuity, unpaid pay and allowances, and Servicemembers' Group Life Insurance (SGLI). To designate persons to be notified in case of emergency and persons authorized to direct disposition of remains.

Routine Uses

Information may be disclosed to the Department of Veterans Affairs, insurance companies, and persons designated on this form. In the event of the service member's death, this information will be used to notify designated persons.

Disclosure

Voluntary; however, failure to provide information may delay notification and payment of benefits to designated beneficiaries.
