Getting Started

SchoolForGood is a comprehensive school management system for tracking members, trainers, classes, and schedules across all 15 sites.

Dashboard

View real-time KPIs and charts showing member distribution across sites, education levels, favorite foods, and class enrollment numbers.

Sites

Browse all 15 campus locations with capacity, contact info, and regional groupings. Each site operates independently with its own trainers and class schedule.

Members

  • Search & Filter — Use the search bar and dropdowns to filter by name, site, or education level
  • Member Detail — Click any member row to view their full profile and enrolled classes
  • Add New Member — Click "Add Member" to register a new member and send a welcome email

Trainers

View the full trainer roster including specialty areas and certifications. Each trainer is assigned to a primary site.

Schedule

  • Visual weekly grid showing all classes Monday through Friday
  • Filter by site to see a single campus schedule
  • Each class card shows time, duration, trainer, room, and current enrollment

Reminders

  • Send Individual — Click "Send" on any pending reminder
  • Send All — Click "Send All Pending" to batch-send all unsent reminders
  • Reminders notify members about their upcoming class times and rooms

Data Model

  • Sites — 15 campuses across the US
  • Members — Students/participants with profile data
  • Trainers — Instructors with specialties
  • Classes — Scheduled courses at each site
  • Enrollments — Member-to-class assignments (30-60 per class)
  • Reminders — Automated class notifications

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Esc — Close modals and dropdowns
